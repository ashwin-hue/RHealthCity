Skill Lab

Rajalakshmi Health City's mannequin-based skills training provides students with realistic, hands-on clinical experience using advanced simulation technology. Our high-fidelity mannequins replicate human physiology, allowing students to practice procedures and emergency scenarios in a safe environment. The program enhances clinical skills, critical thinking, and teamwork while receiving expert faculty guidance. Integrated into our curriculum, this training ensures students gain confidence and competence before treating real patients, preparing them to deliver exceptional healthcare.
